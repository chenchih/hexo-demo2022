6 Print List

There are couple of ways to print list

test = ['A' , 'B', 'C' , 'D']

  • Method 1: using for loop
  • Method 2: *listname unpack list
    We can use without for loop to unpack list

    print(*test) # A B C D

Print new line:

print(*test, sep=’\n’)
